About School

We believe that every child is unique and every child has an untapped store house of talents, skills and capabilities Asia Pacific International School, Bhopal endeavors to provide a conducive environment to identify the potential of the children, chisel their talents, stimulate their imagination and open the doors of their mind to the road of creativity and innovativeness.

The Asia Pacific International School has been conceived as a solution to the growing need of Global schools in India. Asia Pacific International School is conceived not just as a school, but also as a platform to nurture excellence in each kid under its umbrella and to cultivate the nation's dreams.

Visiting Asia Pacific International School

  • We welcome parents to visit Asia Pacific International School, Bhopal. However, please follow the time-schedule for smooth functioning of the school.
  • All visitors will enter the school premises with valid entry pass/badge.
  • All visitors must report to the school office on arrival from where they will be escorted to the designated area.
  • Visitors are not permitted to bring cameras, fire arms or any weapon (even if licensed) on to the school campus.
  • The vehicles must be parked in the parking area. In the interest of safety and cleanliness, everyone is expected to walk on the campus.
  • Parents and Guardians are not allowed to see the students or teachers without the permission of Principal.
