Procedure to Take Admission in Class 11th
in Asia Pacific International School

It is the most exciting phase in the life of students when they go to high school. Because they get to choose their favorite stream. Students get well-detailed exposure to the subjects they love the most. If you have passed the 10th standard, it is most advisable to choose your stream as early as possible if you have not already.

But, if you have chosen your stream, this post is for you. In this post, we are going to discuss the procedure to take admission in class 11th in Asia Pacific International School.

The school offers higher education in commerce and science streams. If you are looking for pursuing higher education in Bhopal MP, Asia Pacific International School provides one of the best high-quality education in the region. The school is equipped with modern laboratories , a library, high-tech classrooms , a science park, a mini Astro observatory, and a herbal park. These facilities are designed to help students nurture a scientific mindset.

Now, let's move toward the admission procedure for class 11th. Step by step we will guide you through the complete process. First of all

When will admission open for class 11th?

The admission process for class 11th starts just after the results of 10th standard are out. Stay tuned to our website for the latest updates about when will admission be open for class 11th in 2022. Alternatively, connect with us on our email ID or contact number from 8 AM to 4 PM on all working days, to get updates about the admission process for class 11th.

What is the process for class 11th admission?

Anyone desirous to take admission in class 11th at the Asia Pacific Internation School should fill up the application form along with the admission kit and submit it to the school counselor during school hours.

You are required to attach

5 copies of recent passport size photographs (4 copies of student +1 parents)

Marksheets of previous standards

Countersigned transfer certificate

ID and Address proof

Birth certificate

The school will inform parents about acceptance of admission within 7 working days through email and telephone. Admission will be confirmed after receipt of school fees payment.

You can avail of the registration form on all working days from 10 AM to 2 PM from the school. Alternatively, connect with us via email or telephone for more details about the admission process. Now, we will move to the next part

Brief detail about class 11th admission 2022

As we discussed earlier, the admission process for the class11th 2022-2023 session is going to commence very soon, just after the results of class 10th. Stay tuned to our website for the latest developments about the admission procedure after the results of class 10. Make all the required documents handy for the admission process till then.

So, that's it about the admission procedure for class 11, feel free to connect with us for more details and queries.

Visiting Asia Pacific International School

  • We welcome parents to visit Asia Pacific International School, Bhopal. However, please follow the time-schedule for smooth functioning of the school.
  • All visitors will enter the school premises with valid entry pass/badge.
  • All visitors must report to the school office on arrival from where they will be escorted to the designated area.
  • Visitors are not permitted to bring cameras, fire arms or any weapon (even if licensed) on to the school campus.
  • The vehicles must be parked in the parking area. In the interest of safety and cleanliness, everyone is expected to walk on the campus.
  • Parents and Guardians are not allowed to see the students or teachers without the permission of Principal.
